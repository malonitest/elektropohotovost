## Google Alerts Setup Instructions

### What to Monitor:
Stay informed about local electrical news, safety alerts, and trending topics in your service area.

### Alerts to Create:

#### 1. Local Emergency News
- **Query:** `"výpadek proudu" Praha OR Beroun OR "Praha-západ"`
- **Frequency:** As-it-happens
- **Sources:** News
- **Language:** Czech
- **Purpose:** Respond quickly to local power outages with blog posts or social media

#### 2. Electrical Safety Alerts
- **Query:** `"elektro havárie" OR "elektrická bezpečnost" Česko`
- **Frequency:** At most once a day
- **Sources:** News
- **Language:** Czech
- **Purpose:** Create content about trending safety topics

#### 3. Electrical Code Changes
- **Query:** `"ČSN" OR "elektro norma" OR "revize elektro" změna`
- **Frequency:** At most once a week
- **Sources:** News, Blogs
- **Language:** Czech
- **Purpose:** Update blog content about regulations

#### 4. Competitor Monitoring (Optional)
- **Query:** `"elektrikář Praha-západ" OR "elektro pohotovost Beroun"`
- **Frequency:** At most once a week
- **Sources:** Web
- **Language:** Czech
- **Purpose:** Stay aware of competitor content and pricing

#### 5. Customer Review Mentions
- **Query:** `"Maloni" "elektro" OR "elektrikář"`
- **Frequency:** As-it-happens
- **Sources:** Blogs, Web
- **Language:** Czech
- **Purpose:** Track brand mentions and respond to reviews

### How to Set Up Google Alerts:

1. Go to: https://www.google.com/alerts
2. Enter your query in the search box
3. Click "Show options" to customize:
   - **How often:** Choose based on urgency
   - **Sources:** Select News, Blogs, or Web
   - **Language:** Czech
   - **Region:** Czechia
   - **How many:** "All results" for comprehensive coverage
   - **Deliver to:** Your email address
4. Click "Create Alert"

### What to Do with Alerts:

**When you receive an alert:**
1. Read the article/news
2. Assess if it's relevant to your customers
3. If yes:
   - Create a blog post responding to the news
   - Share safety tips related to the incident
   - Update existing content if regulations changed
   - Share on social media with your expert commentary
4. If it mentions your business:
   - Respond professionally
   - Thank customers for reviews
   - Address any concerns promptly

### Review Schedule:
- **Weekly:** Check all alert emails on Monday morning
- **Monthly:** Review alert settings and adjust queries if needed
- **Quarterly:** Evaluate which alerts are most valuable and remove low-value ones

## Tracking Content Freshness

### Blog Post Update Priority:

**High Priority (Update within 1 week):**
- Posts ranking on page 2 (positions 11-20)
- Posts with outdated information (price changes, regulation changes)
- Posts with >50% traffic drop in last quarter

**Medium Priority (Update within 1 month):**
- Posts not updated in >6 months
- Seasonal content before relevant season
- Posts with good traffic but outdated screenshots/examples

**Low Priority (Update within 3 months):**
- Evergreen content performing well
- Posts with minimal traffic
- Posts about specific cases/examples that remain relevant

### Update Template:

When updating old content:
1. Update `updatedAt` field in frontmatter to current date
2. Add "Aktualizováno [month] [year]" note at top if major changes
3. Review all facts, prices, and links for accuracy
4. Add new information or examples
5. Optimize for current keyword trends
6. Check and update internal links
7. Rebuild site and verify changes
